# Head of Social

**Team:** Growth | **Reports to:** Austin Tedesco, Head of Growth | **Location:** Remote (NYC HQ)

---

## The role

Every is the only subscription you need to stay at the edge of AI. We publish daily for 130K+ readers, build five AI products (Spiral, Cora, Sparkle, Monologue, Plus One), and run AI transformation consulting for Fortune 500 companies.

We need a Head of Social to shape how AI-powered and agent-native workflows drive social media growth. You'll lead the strategy at Every across social platforms, while owning how business initiatives like editorial content, product launches, and marketing campaigns integrate with social channels.

The team at Every lives at the bleeding edge of what's possible with AI. The people who work here are trusted by the top players in the space, from the teams building the models to the companies deploying them. You'll build an industry-leading, AI-forward approach to social fueled by our core loop: We live in the future, we write what we see, we build what's missing, we teach what works.

## What you'll do

**Own channel strategy and growth**
- Define what Every looks and sounds like on each platform — and why
- Set goals, build the content calendar, and drive measurable growth across all social channels
- Decide what formats, cadences, and approaches work for each platform

**Integrate social with the business**
- Turn product launches, editorial pieces, camps, and company milestones into social moments that compound
- Build the systems that make social a growth channel

**Empower the team**
- Build playbooks, templates, and workflows that make it easy for the internal Every team to create powerful social posts
- Use AI agents and tools to scale social operations

## What we're looking for

**3-7 years in social media strategy**
- You've owned a social presence end-to-end — strategy, content, execution, results.
- You think in channels and systems, not just posts. You know why something works on X but dies on LinkedIn.
- You lead with strategy, not just execution. You can explain why a channel matters, what the goal is, and how you'll measure it.

**Deeply AI-pilled**
- You're already using AI tools daily. You have strong opinions about what's good and what's not.
- You understand the AI landscape well enough to create content that resonates with frontier-level builders.
